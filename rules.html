Rules

Team Guidelines

Teams must have a minimum of two vocalists, but other than that there are no requirements to how many vocalists or non-singing staff members one's team can have. However, teams can only have up to 12 members. Vocalists who also take staff roles only take up one member slot.

Teams must elect a team leader as the main point of contact for the CB staff, as well as a backup leader in case the regular leader is unavailable.

Teams may be altered freely before round two takes place, but the HCCB staff must be notified each time. If you fail to comply/contact the HCCB staff there may be a small deduction to your final score.

You may only join one team. However, if your team is eliminated, you are allowed to join another team afterwards.

Anon groups are allowed, however, when registering you must write your teams' anon and real aliases. Only the HCCB staff will know your real aliases.

All team members must have an active social media account which is not private.
