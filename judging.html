Scoring Rubric

Affection System

The affection system is here to help you gauge a particular capture target’s fondness towards you via affection points. The more points you have, the more likely they will respond positively to you and your advances. Affection points will not affect your entry’s score whatsoever, it is purely for obtaining items and story purposes.

During entry submission you will be asked which capture target you wish to pursue. You may only choose one character and you will only gain or lose affection with that character. You can switch your target in the next round, but we strongly advise against it. Should you choose to switch, the affection points you gained from the previous round will not carry over.

You can gain affection points from your chosen capture target in 3 ways:

  • Incorporating one of the capture target’s likes into your entry
  • Completing secret missions found in the QUESTS tab
  • Participating in twitter events.

However, do take caution while trying to gain affection points. Accidentally including a dislike in your entry; taking on the wrong missions; or selecting the wrong answer during the twitter events may result in a loss of affection. Be sure to get to know your capture target well over in lore and character bios if you wish to win them over and receive a good ending.
