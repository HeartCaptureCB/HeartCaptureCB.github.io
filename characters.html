Nahito Seijou

CV: Vozzi

Class: 2-B | Club: School Newspaper (forced into it by Ru)

A laid back but kind boy. He is childhood friends with Ru Kaguya and they remain close friends. Nahito is sometimes referred to as the “Ru-sitter” as he can often be seen helping her with daily tasks or keeping her from jumping to conclusions. Nahito often helps Ru with her interviews for the newspaper club and assists in cleaning up after volleyball practice. Contrary to popular belief, they are not dating.

Ru Kaguya

CV: canna 星の花

Class: 2-B | Club: School Newspaper

A friendly gyaru girl who befriends the main character with her friend, Nahito. Ru is part of the newspaper club and she can regularly be observed promoting it to anyone who will listen. She is childhood friends with Nahito Seijou and they remain close friends, spending most of their time together. Contrary to popular belief, they are not dating.
