HAKU-CHAN'S MAILBOX

About Haku-chan

The anonymous writer of a popular column in the school paper known as “Haku-chan’s Confession Column.” No one knows her true identity, or if she’s even a student at all. Regardless, her column is the most popular part of the school paper and receives a large amount of submissions each week.

Haku-chan’s regular column consists of a Q&A section, a note from her, and her confession tip of the week. Special editions include how to make the best sweets (the week before valentine’s day), gift ideas (before white day and christmas) and sometimes, matchmaking columns.

Any student wishing for a flourishing love life simply has to read her column and take on her advice. Stay tuned - you don’t want to miss any crucial information!
