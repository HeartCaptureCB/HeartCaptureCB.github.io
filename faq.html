FAQ

Feel free to contact us on Twitter @HeartCaptureCB.

We’re happy to answer questions or concerns you may have.

Q1: Why do we need a Team Leader? Can’t anyone from our team DM you?

A: Technically, yes. However, it is much easier to have your team leader to DM us if there are any problems/questions rather than having multiple people from your team contacting us with the same message. It stops the repetitiveness and it also decreases the amount of confusion it can cause for the organisers. However, if your leader is MIA then feel free to reelect another one and contact us about the change.

Q2: Do we have to join the Discord server?

A: Nope! It is optional to join the server, important details will be posted on both Twitter and Discord. However, it is recommended to join our server as there may be times where we do not post on both platforms due to forgetting or something alike.

Q3: Can my group members/all of us be anon?

A: Yes, as long as us organisers know your true identity(ies), you definitely can go anon!

Q4: Can we make a promo?

A: You are free to make a promo, however, there will not be any bonuses available.
