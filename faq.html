FAQ

Last updated on Jan 6 2021.

Feel free to contact us on Twitter @HeartCaptureCB.

We’re happy to answer questions or concerns you may have.

Q1: Why do we need a Team Leader? Can’t anyone from our team DM you?

A: Technically, yes. However, it is much easier to have your team leader to DM us if there are any problems/questions rather than having multiple people from your team contacting us with the same message. It stops the repetitiveness and it also decreases the amount of confusion it can cause for the organisers.

Q2: Do we have to join the Discord server?

A: Nope! It is optional to join the server, important details will be posted on both Twitter and Discord.

Q3: Can my group members/all of us be anon?

A: Yes, as long as us organisers know your true identity(ies), you definitely can go anon!

Q4: Can we make a promo?

A: You are free to make a promo, however, there will not be any bonuses available.

Q5: Can we use the official character sprites and/or GUIs in our entries?

A: You are free to download and use the GUI designs and CB Logo in your entries, with appropriate credits linking to https://twitter.com/Wisperlings. However, you may NOT use the official character art sprites - we would rather you came up with creative methods to incorporate your capture target instead!
