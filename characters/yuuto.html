Yuuto is the 2nd year representative of the student council and the youngest person in the literature club. He spends most of his time in the library and is often seen being dragged out to other places by his friends. That is... by the friends he does have at least.

Yuuto finds it hard to be honest with himself and others, especially in regards to his feelings. Even when he has good intentions it all just comes out wrong. Add in the fact that he often speaks without thinking and you can see why he has such a hard time keeping friends around.

Will you be able to befriend him and untangle his convoluted words?
