Emi is currently the captain of the volleyball team, as well as an active member of the track team and cheer squad. She has tried her hand at many other sports, such as gymnastics, tennis and skating, and is generally good at all of them. Her friendly nature and athletic skills have led to her being one of the most popular girls in the entire school.

Will you get swept up in her waves of enthusiasm or will you rise above the rest and catch her fancy?
