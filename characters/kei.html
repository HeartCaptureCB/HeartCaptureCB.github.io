Kei, the infamous “Ice Queen” of the school, is a blunt and strait-laced girl. She rarely shows her emotions and often speaks her mind without a second thought. Her intelligence is unrivalled among her peers and her streak of perfect scores since middle school is a new record for this school. This academic excellence earned her a spot on the student council from year 1.

However, as one might expect, her uptight personality and inexpressiveness makes her difficult to approach. Her classmates see her as a nuisance who can’t read the air and ruins their fun. As such, she has few friends and often spends her time alone.

But is that really all there is to her? Perhaps you could be the one to melt her icy heart and see the other side.
