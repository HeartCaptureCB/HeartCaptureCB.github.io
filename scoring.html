Organisers

Art by keitherine_

Kasumi

Hello everyone! I’m Kasumi, one of the organisers and writers for HCCB! Incidentally, I’m also the one who came up with the CB’s concept :)

I’ve participated in around ten different CBs since I’ve become a youtaite and I’ve been staff for quite a few, too! HCCB is the second CB I’ve hosted so far, but to be honest I think it’s probably the most complicated thing I’ll ever organise. Regardless, I hope you all enjoy the competition and I can’t wait to see what you guys can make!

Lunarveil

Hi everyone! I’m Lunarveil — one of the organizers as well as animation staff for HCCB. I’m a vocalist and animator who loves everything cute and sweet! I also love taking on CBs to have a fun time meeting new people and growing alongside friends. As one of the organizers, I’m super excited to see people create amazing groups and put together heart-capturing entries. And as an animator behind-the-scenes, I cant wait to bring our lovely cast of characters and their stories to life for you all to enjoy. Cheers!
